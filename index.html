Illuvium: Beyond Wave 3 is Coming!

Illuvium Beyond Wave 3 launches on November 21, 2024, with visual overhauls, new rewards, and secondary wallet support. Here’s everything you need to know!

If you were one of those people heavily invested in Illuvium: Beyond, you might have been feeling that the Illuvium team is only paying attention to Zero, Arena (with Gauntlet and Leviathan), and Overworld.

Well, here’s some good news for you: the Illuvium team hasn’t forgotten Beyond! Check out Kieran’s latest post on the Illuvium Discord:

Visit Us

Yes, Beyond Wave 3 is coming! Save the date: November 21, 2024.

That’s especially since it’s packed with exciting updates, exclusive rewards, and fresh visuals that’ll make your collection more rewarding than ever. Get ready, because this is the upgrade you've been waiting for!

What Is Illuvium: Beyond?

So, just in case you have no idea what we’re talking about, Illuvium: Beyond is a unique aspect of the Illuvium universe, focusing on collectible NFT Illuvitars (one of the best parts about Web3 games). Players can collect, customize, and showcase these digital avatars, which are not only visually appealing but also deeply connected to the broader Illuvium experience.

Illuvitars come with customizable accessories, backgrounds, and finishes, offering players the chance to create their ultimate combination. Whether you’re in it for the art or the rewards, Illuvium: Beyond brings a whole new level of engagement to the world of NFTs.

(Want to learn more about Beyond and why you should get into it? Check out our Beyond article here.)

Now, on to what you can expect for the upcoming Wave 3!

Album and Collection Visual Overhaul

Read Post

With Wave 3, you’ll get a complete redesign of the album and collection interfaces, breathing new life into your Illuvitars. The new visuals will make the collection process even more rewarding, with fresh artwork that feels more dynamic and immersive.

Account-Bound Rewards

Your journey through Illuvium: Beyond will now be more closely integrated with the rest of the Illuvium universe. Wave 3 brings account-bound rewards, which allow you to unlock exclusive items like battle boards, skins, emotes, and drone blasts. These rewards are tied to your progression and make your experience in the game even more personal.

(Want a guide on how to get started playing Illuvium? Check out our guide here!)

Secondary Wallet Support

If you’re managing multiple wallets, you’ll be happy to hear that Wave 3 will introduce read-only secondary wallet support. This means you can fill your album from a secondary wallet! While the bonding and burning mechanics won't be included at launch, expect additional wallet functionalities in future updates.

Enhanced Reward Structure for Legacy Players

To thank long-time players for their patience during the pause in leaderboard rewards, Wave 3 will offer 3x rewards for a limited time. More details will be shared during the upcoming town hall (more on this later!) on how this reward structure will work.

Wave 3 Showcase Video

The Illuvium team plans to release a Wave 3 showcase video. It’s still in development and it’s set to be released before the official launch of Beyond Wave 3. This video will give players a sneak peek into all the updates and features, so make sure you stay tuned for a thrilling reveal when the video comes out!

Final Thoughts

Aren’t all these exciting? Of course, you likely have questions about Illuvium: Beyond’s Wave 3. If yes, make sure you stop by the upcoming Town Hall this Friday! Kieran and the Illuvium team will dive deeper into the features and address any community inquiries. Don’t miss out on this chance to get your questions answered directly from the team, okay?

In the meantime, make sure to block off November 21, 2024 for the official release! Whether you’re a long-time collector or just jumping in, this Wave is set to give us all a brand new experience that we’re all sure to enjoy!
